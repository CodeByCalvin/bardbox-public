## About

A tool that allows Dungeon Masters to bring their D&D games to life by organising their music into playlists, tagging, creating soundscape presets and much more.

## ✨ Current Features

- **📁 Audio File Management** - Upload and organise your music and sound effects
- **🎼 Music & SFX Separation** - Organize tracks into music and sound effects categories
- **▶️ Built-in Audio Player** - Play your tracks directly within the application
- **📋 Track List View** - View and manage your uploaded audio files
- **🎚️ Playback Controls** - Standard audio controls for seamless playback

## 🚀 Future Features

- **🏷️ Track Tagging** - Tag tracks for easy searching and filtering (e.g., "combat", "tavern", "suspense")
- **📝 Custom Playlists** - Create and manage custom playlists for different scenarios
- **🎭 Custom Scenes** - Build soundscape presets combining music and SFX for specific scenes
- **🔍 Advanced Search & Filtering** - Quickly find the perfect track for any moment
- **💾 Save & Load Sessions** - Persist your library and playlists between sessions
- **🎲 Random Track Selection** - Shuffle through appropriate tracks for spontaneous moments

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **React 19**
- **TypeScript**
- **Vite**
- **Tailwind**
- **Radix UI**
